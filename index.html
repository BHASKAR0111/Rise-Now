Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about RiseNow and starting your comeback.

Is RiseNow completely free?

+

Yes — 100% free. All 7 tools, career paths, resources, and stories on RiseNow are completely free. We curate only free learning platforms (Google, Kaggle, freeCodeCamp etc.) and will always keep the core platform free.

I have no experience. Where do I start?

+

Take the Skill Assessment Quiz first — it gives you a personalized recommendation. Then open the Roadmap Builder for your chosen path and start the 30-Day Challenge. No experience needed.

How long does it take to switch careers?

+

Realistically 3–9 months with consistent daily effort (1–2 hrs/day). The success stories on this platform show people landing jobs in 6–8 months using only free resources. Speed depends on consistency, not talent.

Do I need a degree for these career paths?

+

No. All career paths on RiseNow are accessible without a traditional degree. Companies today hire based on skills and portfolio — not certificates. A strong GitHub profile and real projects matter more than most degrees in tech roles.

Does my data save between visits?

+

Yes — all your habit tracker progress, 30-day challenge completion, and job tracker data are saved locally in your browser (localStorage). No login required. It persists as long as you use the same browser and don't clear site data.

Can I submit my own success story?

+

Absolutely! Use the Contact form below to share your story. If it can inspire others, we'll feature it on RiseNow. Your comeback could be exactly what someone else needs to read today.
