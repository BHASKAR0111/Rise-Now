Tailor for Every Job
Never send a generic resume. Mirror the exact keywords from the job description — ATS systems scan for them. Spend 10 min customizing per application.
📊
Quantify Everything
Replace vague claims with numbers. Instead of "improved performance", write "improved dashboard load time by 40%". Numbers stand out instantly.
⚡
Strong Action Verbs
Start every bullet with a power verb: Built, Developed, Analyzed, Led, Reduced, Increased, Automated, Designed. Avoid "Responsible for" or "Helped".
📏
1 Page Rule
Less than 3 years experience? Keep it to 1 page. Recruiters spend avg 7 seconds per resume. Make every line count — remove fluff, keep impact.
🔤
ATS-Safe Formatting
Use simple fonts (Calibri, Arial). No tables, graphics, or headers/footers — ATS can't read them. Use standard section names: Experience, Education, Skills.
🔗
Links That Work
Add GitHub, LinkedIn, and Portfolio at the top. Recruiters click these. Make sure your GitHub has pinned projects with good READMEs before you apply.
